# **App Name**: CorpoLeve Quiz

## Core Features:

- Weight Loss Quiz: Interactive quiz to assess user's weight loss challenges and lifestyle, using the questions provided in the prompt. Generates a personalized analysis of their relationship with food and potential roadblocks.
- Personalized Diagnosis: AI tool that analyzes the user's quiz responses to deliver a diagnosis of potential metabolic blocks caused by the food industry. Focuses on how processed foods affect their body.
- Customized Recommendations: Provide tailored advice and suggest the right recipes and resources. LLM evaluates the user's answers in the quiz, compares it against existing transformation cases and then recommends the resources those people used. 
- Recipe Presentation: Presents 200+ recipes in a visually appealing and easily navigable format.
- Bonus Content Delivery: Deliver bonus content such as the 'Anti-Falso Saudável' guide, 'Antídoto de Emergência,' 'Sobremesas que Enganam o Cérebro,' and 'Manual da Economia no Mercado'.
- Transformation Showcase: Display user transformation stories and visual proofs using a carousel format, linking to images and testimonials to enhance credibility and motivation.
- FAQ Section: Addresses common questions and concerns, reassuring potential users about the practicality and effectiveness of the recipe code, ensuring user confidence.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A vibrant and fresh lime green (#A6D93D) to evoke health and natural vitality.
- Background color: Light and airy off-white (#F7F7F2), providing a clean, uncluttered feel.
- Accent color: A sunny yellow-orange (#FFB347) for CTAs and important highlights, signaling energy and positivity.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif, providing a balance of modernity and warmth that complements the overall design.
- Use simple, clean icons to represent different sections of the recipe, bonus content, and user benefits.
- A clean, straightforward layout focusing on readability and ease of navigation. The layout will feature distinct sections for quiz, diagnosis, recipes, bonus content, transformation stories, and FAQ.
- Subtle transitions and animations when navigating between quiz questions, displaying personalized diagnoses, and presenting user transformations. Aim for a smooth, engaging, and motivational user experience.
